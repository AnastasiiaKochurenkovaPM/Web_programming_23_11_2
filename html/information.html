DO I NEED A VISA TO VISIT UKRAINE?



Citizens of EU and EEA countries, as well as citizens of the USA, Canada, the UK, Turkey, Saudi Arabia, Oman, UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, and other countries — in total 87 countries — do not need a visa to enter Ukraine as tourists. Citizens of 52+ countries can apply for an e-visa. You can check if you need a visa to Ukraine and visa requirements here.

TRAVEL IN UKRAINE



Convenient domestic flights and trains (both overnight and fast day-trains) provide a comprehensive network of connections between Ukraine’s cities and allow the traveler easy access to all parts of the country.

Ukrainian International Airlines (UIA), SkyUp, and Windrose conduct domestic flights between Ukraine’s major cities, so it’s more than easy to visit Kyiv, Lviv, Odesa, Kharkiv, Dnipro, Kherson, and Zaporizhzhia.

As for the trains in Ukraine, you can book train tickets online, in English by visiting the Ukrainian Railways website.

Another option is to rent a car and explore Ukraine by road. Major international car rental services are present in Ukraine, such as Hertz, Europcar, Sixt, Avis, and others.
