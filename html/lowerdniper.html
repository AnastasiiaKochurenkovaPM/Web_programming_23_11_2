LOWER DNIEPER


The Lower Dnieper is the valley of the Dnieper from the island of Khortytsia to the Dnieper estuary. It occupies part of the territory of Dnipropetrovsk, Zaporizhia, Kherson and to some extent Donetsk regions. Recently, in national opinion polls, the term Lower Dnieper is used to unite Dnipropetrovsk and Zaporizhia regions - two frontline regions that are purely geographically larger than Donbass, deserve to be considered the South-East of the country. This is a macro-region to which a large number of migrants from Donetsk and Luhansk regions have moved in the last year in search of a zone of near peace. The nearest - both territorially and mentally.

Khortytsia



Khortytsia is a unique place, shrouded in legends, myths and legends of different eras. Here rise to the earth's surface granites, which are more than 2.5 billion years old, it is here are the mounds - the same age as the Egyptian pyramids and ancient temples - cromlechs. This place of strength, where thousands of people come, will be recharged with vital energy, and "Zaporozhye Sich" is a symbol of the era of Cossack freedom, military prowess and identity of the Ukrainian people.

Central Park of Culture and Rest "Oak Grove"



This recreation park is located in the Aleksandrovsky district. Zaporozhye. It was founded on May 1, 1959. Its total area is more than 57 hectares, of which 5 are the nature reserve fund. In addition to oak trees, you can see a variety of different flora in the forest. One of the most famous trees here was the "Makhno oak", which was located closer to the side of the city. The tree withered and was eradicated in the 1970s. Now this place is quite popular among residents and guests of Zaporozhye. There is an artificial reservoir, on which two bridges are built. There are many interesting places for leisure activities on the territory: playgrounds, an amusement park, a cafe, a restaurant, a catamaran boat rental place. This is a fairly popular place for celebrating memorable dates and holding thematic photo shoots. Also, sports competitions, master classes, concerts and the like are held here.
